--- question: What kinds of board games are at EGG? --- You won't find board games like Monopoly, Scrabble, or Candy Land at EGG (unless you bring them, that is). Instead you'll find board games like Small World, Settlers of Catan, Terra Mystica, King of Tokyo, Trajan, and many more!
